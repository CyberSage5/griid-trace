# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in griid-trace a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Report violations to conduct@griid-trace.xyz. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
