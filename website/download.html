Verify Downloads

Always verify the integrity of downloaded files using SHA256 checksums.

minisign signature

minisign -Vm SHA256SUMS -P <public-key>

Public key published in AUDIT.md

Linux/macOS

sha256sum -c SHA256SUMS

Windows (PowerShell)

Get-FileHash -Algorithm SHA256 filename.exe
